About Me

My name is Eugene Moses and I am a current Senior at the University of Missouri - Columbia, set to graduate in the Spring of 2024. My academic journey has been a dynamic one, navigating through various colleges before finding my academic home in the College of Arts and Science. I am pursuing a major in Communication with a specialized emphasis in mass media.

Within this field, my focus extends to creative output, writing, and style. A combination that profoundly influences communication across all media platforms. It might not be immediately evident, but much like the thoughtful creation of a piece of art, every message you encounter has undergone a meticulous thought process before being translated into words, shared on social media, or transformed into a script for actors.

As I navigate my final year, I am enthusiastic about applying these skills to real-world scenarios, contributing to the nuanced world of communication and media. Feel free to explore my academic journey and the insights gained through the years I have studied. As I am not an Art major of any sort, everything has always had an influence from it.
